tBLASTn matches to genes from other locus references that were found outside the locus region of your assembly.
Several of the locus genes are homologous to genes found in other locations in the genome e.g. the rml or man genes. It is very common for Kaptive to find a small number of ‘other’ locus genes outside the locus region of the assembly. These are not a cause for concern and do not negatively impact the confidence of a locus match. However, if Kaptive finds a large number of genes (more than 5 or 6) the results should be treated cautiously.
